Ancient Cities

Ancient cities are urban statements which have been built more than thousands of years ago. Those cities shows the historical values and the political, economic, religious and cultural background of those particular eras.

Anuradhapura: Anuradhapura is the first capital of Sri Lanka and it was as the capital of Sri Lanka from 4th century BCE to 11th Century BC. City has a history of about more than 2000 years. Currently Anuradhapura is an UNESCO World Heritage.

  • • Samadhi Buddha Statue
  • • Twin Pond
  • • Jethawanaramaya
  • • Ruwanwelimaha Seya
  • • Thuparamaya
  • • Abhayagiri Monastry
  • • Isurumuniya Temple
  • • Mirisavetiya Temple
  • • Archeological Museum

Polonnaruwa: Polonnaruwa is the 2nd Kingdom of Sri Lanka from 11th to 13th Century. Due to the political stability and the rise of King Vijayabahu the 1st the capital of Sri Lanka moved to Anuradhapura to Polonnaruwa.

  • • Royal Palace and Audience Hall
  • • Gal Vihara
  • • Rankoth Vehera
  • • Polonnaruwa Vatadage
  • • Lankatilaka Temple
  • • Thuparama Gedige
  • • Statue of Parakramabahu I
  • • Archaeological Museum
