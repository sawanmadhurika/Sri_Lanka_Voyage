Jaffna Culture

Jaffna is the capital city of Northern Province, Sri Lanka. Jaffna was affected by the 30 years of Civil War and it was under the rule of Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam. The Civil War was ended in 2009 but Sri Lankan Army succeeded to re-capture Jaffna in 1995. Jaffna is rich in Tamil Culture because most Tamil population in Sri Lanka lives in Jaffna. Visitors can see so many sightseeing which shows the Tamil Culture and the Civil War related sites as well. Visitors can enjoy authentic Jaffna cuisines like Jaffna Crabs Curry, Jaffna Mutton Curry, Vaada, Dosai and Palmyra related cuisines.
